Common Sense

A multiplayer game to see if you and your friends share common sense!

Email me at jacobvsargent@gmail.com with comments or questions.

Best experienced with 3 or more players.

Host a Game Join a Game

How to Play

  1. Host the game on a laptop (Hooking up to TV recommended!)
  2. Players join using their own devices (phones, laptops, etc.)
  3. Each round presents a question like "What COLOR and TASTE are the MOST HAPPINESS?"
  4. Players choose their answers from dropdown menus for each sense
  5. After everyone locks in their answers, find out if you all share "common sense"
  6. You get points for the senses that you share in common with others
