"Fishing Frenzy" Song for Blooket.com

In the fall of '22, I reached out to popular Education Game website Blooket.com with a proposal. I'd remake a song for one of their games, Fishing Frenzy, and they put it in the game. The song was accepted and will be put into the production build in the Summer of 2023.
